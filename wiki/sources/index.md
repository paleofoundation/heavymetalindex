Each source cited in the Heavy Metal Index has its own page. The source page records the bibliographic metadata, the evidence tier, a terse summary of methods and findings, the key quantitative results, and the wiki pages that cite the source. Cite keys follow the convention `author-year-slug`, lowercase and hyphenated, with global uniqueness.

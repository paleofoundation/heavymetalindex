# FDA 2026 — Infant Formula Toxic Elements Special Survey

## TL;DR

This FDA special-survey table reports total arsenic, lead, cadmium, and mercury results for infant formula samples tested in FY2023-FY2025. It is a priority source for Category 1 formula pages because results are expressed as prepared for feeding, enabling comparison across formula formats after careful classification.

## Key numbers

- The arsenic table represents 312 infant formula samples tested in FY2023-FY2025.
- The lead table represents 312 infant formula samples tested in FY2023-FY2025.
- The cadmium table represents 312 infant formula samples tested in FY2023-FY2025.
- The mercury table represents 312 infant formula samples tested in FY2023-FY2025.
- Total arsenic LOQs expressed as prepared for feeding ranged from 0.2 to 0.4 ppb; LODs ranged from 0.05 to 0.2 ppb.
- Lead LOQs expressed as prepared for feeding ranged from 0.1 to 0.4 ppb; LODs ranged from 0.04 to 0.1 ppb.
- Cadmium LOQs expressed as prepared for feeding ranged from 0.1 to 0.3 ppb; LODs ranged from 0.04 to 0.1 ppb.
- Mercury LOQs expressed as prepared for feeding ranged from 0.08 to 0.2 ppb; LODs ranged from 0.03 to 0.08 ppb.

## Methods (brief)

FDA collected infant formula samples as part of a special survey that also included toddler drinks. Results were converted to prepared-for-feeding values using label preparation instructions, with no assumed contribution from added water.

## Limitations

The table is a structured dataset source embedded in PDF form. Product-page p-values require row extraction, product-format classification, non-detect handling, and separation of infant formula from toddler drinks where applicable.

## Implications

Certification: Priority U.S. A-tier formula dataset for aggregate distributions and current-market context.

Courses: Useful example of prepared-for-feeding normalization and explicit LOD/LOQ disclosure.

App: Supports U.S. formula risk estimates and evidence-gap closure for total arsenic, lead, cadmium, and total mercury.

Microbiome: No direct microbiome endpoint.

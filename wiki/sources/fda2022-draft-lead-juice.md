# FDA 2022 Draft Lead-in-Juice Guidance

## Summary

FDA's April 2022 lead-in-juice guidance is draft guidance and not for implementation. If finalized, it would replace the current 50 ppb Juice HACCP lead context with 10 ppb for single-strength apple juice and 20 ppb for other single-strength juices and juice blends.

## Key numbers

| Juice scope | Draft lead action level | HMI ppb value | Status |
| --- | ---: | ---: | --- |
| Single-strength apple juice | 10 ppb | 10 ppb | Draft; not for implementation |
| Other single-strength juices and juice blends | 20 ppb | 20 ppb | Draft; not for implementation |

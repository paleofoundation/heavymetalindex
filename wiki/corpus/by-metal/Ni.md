As is classified as a group 1 carcinogen and of the three toxic elements analyzed in this study, it had the highest concentrations. However, chemical toxicity varies with As species, and therefore species analysis is required to determine the actual toxicity of As. Inorganic As (As<sup>3+</sup> and As<sup>5+</sup>) is more harmful than organic As (AsC, AsB, DMA, and MMA). In particular, AsB is considered nontoxic. Therefore, speciation of As is required to clarify the health risks of As from food.

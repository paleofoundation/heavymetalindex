Supply-chain pages describe the paths by which heavy metals enter the food system. Coverage includes soil geochemistry and agricultural uptake, irrigation and process water, fertilizers and soil amendments, food-contact equipment and materials, packaging, and storage conditions. Pages link to the downstream [ingredients](/ingredients) and [products](/products) affected, and to the [regulations](/regulations) governing inputs.

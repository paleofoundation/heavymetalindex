# Zinc

_Stub page. Zinc is an essential nutrient and not an HMTc toxic-metal analyte, but it appears in infant-formula elemental surveys and toxicology sources that also cover cadmium and lead. Astolfi et al. 2021 estimated zinc intake from powdered infant formula and reported health risk indexes around 5 percent of the cited upper limit.

Eight metals are in scope for this wiki. Each has a dedicated page describing its toxicology, typical exposure routes through food, food sources linked to [ingredients](/ingredients), regulatory limits linked to [regulations](/regulations), analytical methods linked to [testing](/testing), and microbiome effects where documented. Arsenic and mercury are maintained under separate pages for the inorganic and total analytical quantities, because the toxicological and regulatory implications of the two species differ sharply.

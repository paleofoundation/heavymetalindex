# Lithium

Lithium is most relevant to this wiki as a metal used in medical therapy for mania and bipolar disorder. [[sources/ufelle2021-metals-chapter|Ufelle & Barchowsky 2021]] attaches lithium to the wiki through its narrow therapeutic index and toxicities involving neuromuscular, central nervous system, cardiovascular, gastrointestinal, renal, and thyroid effects.

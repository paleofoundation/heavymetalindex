# Briefings

Briefings are interpretive Heavy Metal Index pages. They synthesize public evidence, regulatory context, and source-method issues without turning the Index into a news blog, brand ranking, or certification-claims site.

Each briefing should be traceable to source pages, regulatory records, or clearly labeled external context. Briefings may explain why a contaminant finding matters; they should not treat an isolated detection as a complete risk assessment.

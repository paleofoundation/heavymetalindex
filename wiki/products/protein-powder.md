## Basis and QA Comparator Notes

Protein-powder QA programs often test dry finished powder and may also test high-risk ingredients such as cocoa, rice protein, pea protein, whey, or mineral premixes. Finished powder, prepared shakes, and ingredient COAs are separate evidence objects unless recipe proportion and dilution are documented.

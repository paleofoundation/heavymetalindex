## Basis and QA Comparator Notes

Wet puree values should not be compared with dry ingredient COAs without moisture normalization. QA teams should compare only like with like: same analyte/species, same basis, same unit, compatible sample preparation, and documented LOQ/LOD or censoring treatment. A COA on a dry ingredient, a finished product as sold, a prepared serving, and a pooled market basket are separate evidence objects.

_This page is a stub. Substantive content lands after the ingest of the EFSA CONTAM Panel scientific opinion on cadmium in food._

The European Food Safety Authority's Panel on Contaminants in the Food Chain set a tolerable weekly intake for cadmium based on renal tubular effects in the general population. The TWI is the EU reference value that member-state regulatory decisions and dietary exposure assessments are built against.

# ATSDR — Minimal Risk Levels for Cadmium

_This page is a stub. Substantive content lands after the ingest of the ATSDR Toxicological Profile for Cadmium._

The Agency for Toxic Substances and Disease Registry publishes Minimal Risk Levels by route of exposure and by duration of exposure. MRLs are used in US public health assessments at hazardous waste sites and in community exposure investigations. ATSDR's cadmium profile is the most comprehensive single-source synthesis of cadmium toxicology produced by a US agency.

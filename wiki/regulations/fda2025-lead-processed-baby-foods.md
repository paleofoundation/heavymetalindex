# FDA 2025 Lead Action Levels for Processed Baby Foods

FDA's January 2025 final guidance sets lead action levels for processed foods intended for babies and young children under two years old. The values loaded into the crosswalk are 10 ug/kg for fruits, non-root vegetables, mixtures, yogurts, custards/puddings, and single-ingredient meats; 20 ug/kg for single-ingredient root vegetables; and 20 ug/kg for dry infant cereals.

The guidance is nonbinding, but FDA states that the action levels reflect levels at which it may regard a food as adulterated when considering enforcement. The values should be treated as regulatory context and potential caps, not as HMTc standards values.

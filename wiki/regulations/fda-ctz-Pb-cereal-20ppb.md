# FDA Closer to Zero — Lead Action Level for Dry Infant Cereals

_This page is a stub. Substantive content lands after the ingest of the FDA guidance document that sets this action level._

Under the Closer to Zero program, FDA has established a separate lead action level for dry infant cereals. The category includes dried cereals without additions and dried cereals containing dried fruits or vegetables (such as apple, banana, or carrot). Dry infant cereal receives its own threshold because it is often the first solid food introduced to an infant and may be the sole solid food consumed for an extended period during a critical developmental window.

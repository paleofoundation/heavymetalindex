# FDA 2020 Inorganic Arsenic Action Level for Infant Rice Cereals

FDA's August 2020 final guidance identifies an action level of 100 ug/kg for inorganic arsenic in infant rice cereals. The guidance applies to all types of infant rice cereals.

This value should not be silently transferred to rice-containing snacks, rice cakes, or mixed meals unless the product matrix is reviewed and explicitly matched.

# EPA IRIS — Inorganic Arsenic Toxicological Review (January 2025)

The January 2025 EPA IRIS Toxicological Review of Inorganic Arsenic (EPA/635/R-25/005Fa, CASRN 7440-38-2) is the finalized US Environmental Protection Agency reassessment of inorganic arsenic toxicity ([[sources/epa-iris-inorganic-arsenic-2025|EPA IRIS iAs 2025]]). It supersedes prior IRIS arsenic values that had been operative for decades, and represents the US federal scientific anchor for inorganic arsenic dose-response that downstream EPA, FDA, and state regulatory actions reference ([[sources/epa-iris-inorganic-arsenic-2025|EPA IRIS iAs 2025]]).

The full quantitative outputs (oral RfD, inhalation RfC, oral cancer slope factor, inhalation unit risk, critical study identification per endpoint) require detailed extraction from the approximately 100+ page review document and are pending consolidation ([[sources/epa-iris-inorganic-arsenic-2025|EPA IRIS iAs 2025]]). Once those values are extracted from the full text, this regulation page will record them with the verbatim agency-stated values.

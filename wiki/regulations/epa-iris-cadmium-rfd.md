# EPA IRIS — Cadmium Oral Reference Dose

_This page is a stub. Substantive content lands after the ingest of the EPA IRIS Toxicological Review for Cadmium._

The Integrated Risk Information System is the US Environmental Protection Agency's database of chronic human health effect values. The oral reference dose for cadmium is the EPA's estimate of a daily oral exposure to the human population that is likely to be without appreciable risk of deleterious effects during a lifetime. It is a toxicity value, not an enforceable standard, but US regulatory and advisory decisions in multiple domains (drinking water, soil cleanup, food) reference it.

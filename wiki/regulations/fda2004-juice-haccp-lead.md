# FDA Juice HACCP Lead Guidance

FDA's Juice HACCP hazards-and-controls guidance refers to a 50 ug/kg lead level for ready-to-drink fruit juices, including fruit nectars, and recommends controls where lead is reasonably likely to occur. FDA's later 2022 lead-in-juice values are still draft, so this older guidance remains relevant legal context.

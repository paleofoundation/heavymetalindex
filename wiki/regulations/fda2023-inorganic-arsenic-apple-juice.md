# FDA 2023 Inorganic Arsenic Action Level for Apple Juice

FDA's June 2023 final guidance identifies an action level of 10 ug/kg for inorganic arsenic in apple juice. The value is apple-juice-specific and should be kept separate from total arsenic values and from non-apple juice categories.

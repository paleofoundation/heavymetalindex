# FDA 2022 Draft Lead Action Levels for Juice

FDA's April 2022 draft guidance proposed lead action levels of 10 ug/kg for single-strength apple juice and 20 ug/kg for other single-strength juices and juice blends. FDA marks this document as draft guidance and not for implementation.

These draft values are useful for standards-development context, but product pages must not present them as final regulatory limits.

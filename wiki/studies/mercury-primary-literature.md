# Mercury Primary Literature Catalog

This page is a citable index of primary research articles related to mercury in food, supply chain, exposure, biomarkers, and remediation. The articles are held privately under raw/studies/ (gitignored per the licensing model documented in [[raw/README]]); each entry below provides the document filename, an inferred title from the filename, and the DOI where extractable.
